Batman: The Dark Knight

The caped crusader battles the Joker to save Gotham City

Storyline

In Gotham City, the Joker, an evil, twisted criminal mastermind, emerges from the shadows and wreaks havoc upon the city. The Dark Knight must confront the Joker to protect Gotham and its citizens.
